Restaurant: Equinox - A Special Dish

Equinox

818 Connecticut Avenue NW
Washington, DC
202-331-8118
- Previously reviewed -
Imagine my delight, when arriving at Equinox one noon, to find the following item on the menu:

"Michael Berman's Favorite Vegetable Arrangement"

Braised Mustard Greens-Fingerline Potatoes-Caramelized Cauliflower-Spaghetti Squash- (the vegetables may vary)

On a number of occasions I had asked Todd Gray to fix me a vegetable plate as an entree. This was the result. Try it.
