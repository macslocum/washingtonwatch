President Obama

With 6 months to go before the general election, the public’s grade for the job President Obama has done so far is lower than that of 6 of the previous Presidents at this point in their first term.

The President’s current job approval rating (April 16-22) in Gallup is 48% approval and 46% disapproval. The following is the list of 8.

  App Dis
Eisenhower 69 19
Clinton 56 37
Reagan 54 36
Nixon 54 37
G Bush (II) 52 45
Obama 50 44 (4/22-24)
G Bush (I) 42 48
Carter 39 50
