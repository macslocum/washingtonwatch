Tea Party and the 2010 Election

35% of likely 2010 voters are Tea Party supporters. [NBC/WSJ 10/10]

18% say they would be more likely to support a Congressional candidate who associates with the Tea Party, while 28% they would be less likely. 51% say it would make no difference. [ABC/WP 10/10]
