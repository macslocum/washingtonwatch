President Bush - Treading Water

President Bush's job approval ratings remain less than modest. Here is the NBC/WSJ report card.

Approve Disapprove
In general 28% 66%
Handling the economy 21% 73%
Handling Iraq 33% 64%


Bush's favorability, at 30% positive in the same survey, is the lowest score he has received during his tenure in the White House.

The LATimes/Bloomberg survey found Bush to have a 23% approval rating. Only Harry Truman, at 22% in February 1952, was lower. [6/08]

There is really nothing more to say.
