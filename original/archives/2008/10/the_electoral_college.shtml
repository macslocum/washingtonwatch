The Electoral College

A review of the electoral vote over the past 4 Presidential elections provides a basis for the reality that John McCain has a higher hill to climb than Barack Obama.

135 electoral votes - Republican won these States in last 4 elections

64 electoral votes - Republican won these States in 3 of last 4 elections

75 electoral votes - Republicans and Democrats each won 2 of last 4 elections

16 electoral votes - Democrat won these states in 3 of last 4 elections

248 electoral votes - Democrats won these states in last 4 elections

Obama is challenging McCain in States with 39 electoral votes out of the 135 which Republicans won in the last 4 elections.

McCain is not challenging Obama in any of the States of the 248 electoral votes that Democrats won in last 4 elections.


Various news organizations maintain electoral vote counts that are periodically reassessed and updated. Each organization has its own formula for deciding which candidate will receive a State's electoral votes.
