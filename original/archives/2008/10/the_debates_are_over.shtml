The Debates Are Over

The debates are over. The Obama/Biden team has generally been anointed as the winner in all 4 contests.

The most watched debate was that between Palin and Biden. It garnered 73.4 million viewers. The most watched Presidential debate was the 2nd of the 3 contests, seen by 66.3 million people.

The final Presidential debate was seen by 59.3 million people. The initial debate between Obama and McCain drew the least amount of attention, with 54.9 million viewers.
