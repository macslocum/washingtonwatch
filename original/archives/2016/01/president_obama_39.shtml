Starting with Eisenhower, the two Presidents who have had the highest approval ratings were George H. W. Bush – 89% -- and George W Bush – 86%. The only other President who broke 80% was John Kennedy.

George H. W. Bush’s high point came when the U.S. freed Kuwait, and for George W. Bush, it was 9/11.

The spread between the high and low for G. W. Bush was 62 points, and between the high and low for G. H. W. Bush was 60 points.
