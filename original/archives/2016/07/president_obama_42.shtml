President Obama

“No matter what you think of his time in office, it’s hard to argue that the human being (Barack Obama) has been anything less than a model of class and dignity.” -Walter F. Mondale

The public’s grading of President Obama’s job approval, as found by Gallup on 7/19/16, is 49% approval/47% disapproval. Obama’s approval rating at the time of his first inauguration was 67% approval/14% disapproval. A year ago it was 47% approval/47% disapproval. [Gallup]
