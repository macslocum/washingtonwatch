The Meaning of Words

Ever since my days growing up in Duluth, Minnesota, I have used two words that I found quite descriptive. It has now been brought to my attention that these two words have not been granted entry to any English language dictionary, the suggestion being that they are, therefore, not words. The words and their meaning follows:

Catsafranas – confused, mixed up, complicated

Difugalty (Difugalties) – a problem
